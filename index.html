About Us

Shenzhen PrimeSol Supply Chain Co., Ltd. is a professional global supply chain solutions provider

Founded in 2010 and headquartered in Shenzhen, we have branches in many countries and regions around the world. We have a professional logistics team and a complete service network.
With years of industry experience, we provide customized logistics solutions for customers, covering international sea freight, air freight, express, cross-border e-commerce logistics and other full-link services.
We always adhere to the concept of "Customer First, Service-Oriented", committed to reducing logistics costs and improving logistics efficiency for customers.
